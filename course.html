Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Python Bootcamp

Do I need any prior programming experience to join?

+

No prior programming experience is required! Our bootcamp is designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to advanced topics. Whether you're a student, working professional, or career switcher, you'll find the curriculum accessible and well-paced.

What is the duration of the bootcamp?

+

The bootcamp is an intensive 4-month program with flexible learning options. We offer both weekday and weekend batches to accommodate working professionals. You can expect to dedicate 10-15 hours per week, including live sessions, projects, and self-paced learning.

What kind of career support do you provide?

+

We provide comprehensive career support including resume building, portfolio development, mock interviews, and direct connections to 1200+ hiring partners. Our placement team works with you one-on-one until you land your desired role. We also offer lifetime career guidance even after course completion.

Are the classes live or pre-recorded?

+

All classes are conducted live with experienced instructors, allowing real-time interaction and Q&A. Sessions are recorded and available for review anytime. You'll also get access to additional pre-recorded content, practice exercises, and supplementary materials on our learning platform.

What projects will I work on during the bootcamp?

+

You'll build 12+ real-world projects including web applications, data analysis dashboards, automation tools, API integrations, and machine learning models. The capstone project simulates a real industry scenario where you'll solve actual business problems. All projects go into your portfolio to showcase to employers.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee from the start date. If you're not satisfied with the bootcamp within the first week, you can request a full refund, no questions asked. After 7 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis depending on circumstances.

Will I receive a certificate after completion?

+

Yes! Upon successful completion of the bootcamp, you'll receive an industry-recognized certificate. This certificate demonstrates your proficiency in Python development and can be shared on LinkedIn, your resume, and portfolio. We also provide project completion certificates for each major project.

What programming tools and software will I need?

+

You'll need a laptop or desktop computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux) with at least 4GB RAM. All software and tools we use are free and open-source, including Python, VS Code, Jupyter Notebooks, and Git. We provide detailed setup guides and technical support to help you get started.
